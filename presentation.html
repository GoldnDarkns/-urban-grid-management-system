The Challenge

Managing Grid Stability Under Extreme Conditions

The Problem:

  • Heat waves → People stay indoors → AC usage spikes
  • High demand → More power generation → Emissions rise
  • High AQI → City must restrict activities → Grid flexibility decreases
  • High demand + Low flexibility = Risk of blackouts

City must balance: Keep people safe (power) vs. Reduce pollution vs. Prevent blackouts
